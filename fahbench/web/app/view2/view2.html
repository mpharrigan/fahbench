FAHBench GPU Benchmarking

FAHBench evaluates the performance of OpenCL devices (like GPUs) using the OpenMM molecular dynamics program. The computational core of FAHBench is nearly identical to Folding@home scientific "cores" that allow donors from around the world to donate computations to solving novel problems in biophysics. A higher score in FAHBench means more effective computations for Folding@home.

FAHBench is maintained by Matthew Harrigan at Stanford University
